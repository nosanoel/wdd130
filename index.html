About Me

I am Idehen Nosayaba of Nigeria. I delight in the playing of football and hold a deep affection for design and learning. I now pursue the study of Web Design and Development, where beauty meeteth purpose. It is my aim to craft websites both fair to behold and fit for use.

Edo State, Nigeria

Edo State, created on August 27, 1991, is located in southern Nigeria with Benin City as its capital. It was part of the former Bendel State before the split into Edo and Delta States. The state is the historic home of the Benin Kingdom, one of Africa’s oldest and most advanced civilizations, known for its bronzes, strong monarchy, and city walls.
