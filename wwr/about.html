History

Established in 2011, White Water Rafting has been creating unforgettable river adventures for over a decade. With a team of highly skilled and experienced guides, we ensure every trip is both safe and exhilarating—perfect for beginners and seasoned rafters alike. .

From our modest beginnings with just two rafts, we’ve grown into the region’s top rafting company, welcoming thousands of happy customers every year. .
