Family Fun Adventure

Perfect for beginners and families, this half-day trip features gentle Class II rapids with plenty of calm water for swimming. Includes safety briefing and light refreshments.

Our most popular full-day adventure featuring Class III-IV rapids. Includes professional photos, lunch by the river, and an experienced guide in each raft.

  • Age Limit: 14+ years
  • Price: $124 per person

  • Difficulty: Expert
  • Age Limit: 18+ years
  • Price: $351 per person
